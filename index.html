First stage

Wake up and make it to your desk with WakeMate (S09).

Open your heart up to an email and journal your struggles with OhLife (S10).

Reduce your time reading Hacker News with RescueTime (W08).

Write your world domination outlines on Workflowy (S10).

Save your short notes with Simplenote (S10).

Work out what customers want with Wufoo (W06).

Whip up a site with Weebly (W07).

To few animations on your site? Try Tumult Hype (W11).

Host your own with Heroku (W08).

Or deploy anything on dotCloud (S10).

Research your contacts with Rapportive (S10).

Compare code and text with Kaleidoscope (W11).

Have your struggle published for all to read, or just find some readings to pass the time with Hyperink (W11).
