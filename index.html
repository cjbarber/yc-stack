What did I learn building this.

YC has funded a lot of companies.

Companies targeting consumers (as opposed to businesses) definitely had a lower chance of still being alive. Why is everyone still attacking the consumer web?

I'm going to take this and expand it.

Firstly - I wasn't in a very serious mood when I built this, hence some of the taglines. These are all awesome companies.
